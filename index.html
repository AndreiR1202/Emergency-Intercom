EMERGENCY INTERCOM

Enya Umanzor and Drew Phillips
started Emergency Intercom out of pandemic boredom with nothing but a small setup in the corner of their kitchen and the comedic chemistry they’ve perfected through nearly a decade of friendship. Now, every Friday, hundreds of thousands of people tune in to hear the best friend duo talk — with the occasional quip from their sound-guy-turned-friend Ky Newman — about things that would get you banned from the dinner table for an hour.
